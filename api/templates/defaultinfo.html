Why Digital Outreach?

Platforms around the web provide different ways for people to access what a museum does, anywhere. Even people who have never heard of Te Papa.

By understanding how these platforms work and who they reach, we find opportunities for the museum's collections and knowledge to have impacts that simply wouldn't happen inside our own (digital) walls.

Each channel we use for digital outreach does this differently. Click around to see what we're doing!

Why the circles?

We think about platforms based on proximity, reach, and control.

  1. Our own websites, with the most direct control over what gets shared (and how)
  2. Sites we directly contribute to, combining our material with other sources
  3. The wider world, with less control but chances to reach more people in new ways
